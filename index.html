About Us


As the club name suggests, we are a group of motorcycle enthusiasts (predominantly Honda riders). The club was founded 25 years ago and currently has 150 members. We meet up at least once a month for weekend spins (weather permitting) and showcase our bikes, exchange stories, help each other with bike issues etc.
Life can be a long, difficult ride but can be better if you don't ride alone. Our club is for all those riders who enjoy the journey more than the goal, for all who don't think where they go when they jump on their bike, but just go.
If riding your bike feels like freedom... if you like to feel the road and leave everything else behind, if you enjoy meeting people and sharing experiences whether good or bad... then put your gear on and come join us.
